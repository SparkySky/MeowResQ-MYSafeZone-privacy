Your Data Subject Rights

MySafeZone is committed to protecting your privacy and giving you control over your personal information.

Your Rights and How to Exercise Them

✅ Right to Access

What it means: You can view and access all your personal data stored in the app.

How to exercise:

  • View your profile information in the app settings
  • Access your incident history
  • Review your location history
  • Or email us at alvinjiahao203@gmail.com to request a complete copy

✅ Right to Correction

What it means: You can update or correct any incorrect personal information.

How to exercise:

  • Edit your profile directly in the app
  • Update your emergency contacts
  • Contact support if you need help: alvinjiahao203@gmail.com

✅ Right to Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")

What it means: You can request that we delete your personal data.

How to exercise:

  • Delete individual incidents from your history in the app
  • Delete your entire account in Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Or email alvinjiahao203@gmail.com with your deletion request

Timeline: All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes.

✅ Right to Data Portability (Export)

What it means: You can download your data in a portable format to transfer to another service.

How to exercise:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy → Export My Data
  • Or email alvinjiahao203@gmail.com to request an export

Format: JSON format including all your personal information, incident reports, and media.

✅ Right to Restriction of Processing

What it means: You can limit how we process your data.

How to exercise:

  • Disable location tracking in app settings
  • Disable audio recording
  • Turn off community sharing
  • Contact us to request specific restrictions: alvinjiahao203@gmail.com

Note: Restricting certain features may limit app functionality.

✅ Right to Object

What it means: You can object to certain types of data processing.

How to exercise:

  • Opt out of analytics in app settings
  • Disable notifications
  • Email us with specific objections: alvinjiahao203@gmail.com

✅ Right to Withdraw Consent

What it means: You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

How to exercise:

  • Revoke location permissions in device settings
  • Revoke camera/microphone permissions in device settings
  • Disable features requiring consent in app settings

Note: Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

Privacy Controls in the App

You have direct control over these privacy settings:

  • Location Tracking: Enable/disable real-time and background location
  • Audio Recording: Control when the app can record audio
  • Emergency Contacts: Manage who receives your emergency alerts
  • Community Sharing: Control whether incidents are shared publicly
  • Notifications: Manage what alerts you receive
  • Data Retention: Configure how long location history is kept

Device Permissions

You can manage these permissions through your device settings:

  • Location access (Foreground & Background)
  • Camera access
  • Microphone access
  • Storage access
  • Notification access

Regional Privacy Rights

European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights:

  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
  • Right to know the legal basis for processing your data
  • Right to know about international data transfers

California Users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what categories of personal information we collect
  • Right to know what personal information is sold (we don't sell data)
  • Right to opt-out of sale (not applicable - we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

No Fees

Exercising your data subject rights is completely free. We do not charge any fees for:

  • Accessing your data
  • Correcting your data
  • Deleting your data
  • Exporting your data

Response Timeline

We will respond to all data subject rights requests within:

  • 30 days for most requests
  • 45 days for complex requests (we will notify you if extension is needed)
